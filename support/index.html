Common Questions

How do I delete my account?
Email us at support@boardsession.app and we'll remove your data within 30 days.

I didn't receive an event invite.
Check your spam folder, or ask the host to resend the invite from the app.

Push notifications aren't working.
Go to your device Settings → Board Session and make sure notifications are enabled.
